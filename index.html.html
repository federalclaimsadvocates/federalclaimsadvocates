Eligibility

You May Qualify If Any
Of These Apply.

You received a RIF notice

With no performance or conduct basis, particularly during a protected leave period.

Contacted during protected leave

Asked to return during paternity, maternity, medical, or FMLA leave without justification.

PSLF eligibility threatened

A RIF notice near your 10-year PSLF completion date with no legitimate basis.

Your family was directly impacted

Financial instability, harm to dependents, or significant upheaval to childcare arrangements resulting from return to office mandates.

Unexplained adverse action

Demotion, harassment, or targeting with no documented performance basis.

Medical or emotional impact

Anxiety, depression, physical illness, or surgical complications directly caused by government action.
